# EMG-002: AI systems have a transparency statement

## Standard

Services that use AI to make or support decisions affecting users SHOULD publish a transparency statement explaining: what AI is used for, how it influences outcomes, what data it was trained on, how to challenge a decision, and known limitations.

## Rationale

- Public trust in government depends on transparency
- Algorithmic decision-making without explanation creates accountability gaps
- Aligns with CDDO AI framework

## What good looks like

- Published transparency statement (linked from service)
- Plain-English explanation of AI's role
- Explanation of human oversight
- Process for challenging AI-influenced decisions
- Statement updated when system changes

## Enforcement

| Mechanism | What is checked | When |
|-----------|----------------|------|
| **Periodic audit** | Reviewed at service assessment for any service using AI in decision support | Service assessment |

**Primary enforcement: Periodic audit.** Reviewed at service assessment for any service using AI in decision support.
